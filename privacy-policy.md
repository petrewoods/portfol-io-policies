# Privacy Policy

## Who we are

## What we collect and why

### Account information
When you sign in with Apple, we store your Apple-issued anonymous user ID, and — on your first sign-in only — your name and email address if you choose to share them. This is used solely to personalise your account and sync your entries across your devices.

### Portfolio entries
The text you dictate is processed entirely on your device before leaving it. Identifiers (names, dates, specific locations, rare diagnoses) are replaced by generalised placeholders by an on-device anonymisation engine before any text is transmitted. **Patient data as you input it is never transmitted and is never visible to the development team or any third party.**

### Usage data
We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics (Firebase Analytics) to understand which features are used and to improve the app. This data cannot identify you or any patient.

### AI processing
The anonymised, de-identified text is transmitted to Google's Gemini API (via Firebase AI Logic) for structuring and curriculum mapping. This transmission contains no patient-identifiable information. Google's data handling is governed by their [AI terms](https://ai.google.dev/terms).

---

## Research use

De-identified, anonymised data generated through the app may be used to improve the service and may contribute to medical education research, including peer-reviewed publication. No patient data and no personally identifiable information is used for these purposes. If data is used in research, it will be in aggregated or anonymised form only.

---

## Data storage and security

Your account data and portfolio entries are stored in Google Firebase Firestore, hosted in the EU (europe-west2 region). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Only you can access your entries; access controls are enforced server-side.

---

## Data retention and deletion

You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. Deletion removes all associated data from our servers within 30 days.
